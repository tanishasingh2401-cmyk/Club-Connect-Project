Kalakrit – Dance & Music

Kalakrit – The Dance & Music Club of our college, is where rhythm meets melody! It provides a creative platform for students who express themselves through the art of dance and music. The club encourages performances across diverse genres — from classical and folk to western, hip-hop, and fusion.
